The Modularization

The specification may be modularized.
It may be reasonable to modularize a specification if it is big or if some kind of organization should be applied.
To create a module:
  1. Go to the Module Editor tab (Figure 4)
  2. Select a pre-existing module by clicking on it at the Modules of Module... area or navigating the module tree at the left side of the screen. If no module was created, just a root module will exist
  3. Right-click at the Modules of Module... area to create a module
